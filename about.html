About us

Our Vision

The Colorado Gifted and Talented Student Board strives to provide ease of learning and prosperity to all Gifted and Talented identified students within Colorado.

Our Mission

The Colorado Gifted and Talented Student Board is committed to obtaining input from gifted students in Colorado to enhance the GT experience.

Education is a basic human right.

The reason students are identified as Gifted and Talented are because their brains are wired so that how we learn and what we do with the content is different from those who are not identified. However, besides identifying students, the education system has neglected GT student needs. This lack of support has negatively impacted GT studentsâ€™ education. Often,the GT curriculum is left up to school and district boards to decide, and those on the boards are not familiar with how GT students work, resulting in the lack of funding and focus on the GT curriculum. Educational board members have the responsibilty to care for GT students as much as they do for non-identified students.

Our Initiative

By providing resources for both students and educators, this website helps prevent negative consequences neglected GT students face and encourages educators to take responsibilty to educate themselves and get familiar with GT in order to make better policies.
